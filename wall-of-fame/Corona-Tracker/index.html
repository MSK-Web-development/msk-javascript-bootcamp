6 Steps Prevention Against Coronavirus

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds with soup and water or use hand sanitiser.

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or mask.

Avoid close contact (1meter or 3 meter) with people who are unwell.

Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell.

Stay informed by watching news & follow the instructions.

If you have fever, cough and difficult breathing, seek medical care early.
